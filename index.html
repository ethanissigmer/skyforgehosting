FAQ

1. Are there any catches?

Unlike some other free hosts, we don't force you to renew servers, and we don't require credit card or phone verification.

2. Are my files safe?

Unless there is corruption, SkyForge Hosting shutting down, or legal requirements, we will never randomly delete your files.

3. How is it so cheap?

We spend less money on addons and support, so support may be slower in some cases, but because of that, we can offer prices 3x cheaper than top hosts.

4. Is my server 24/7?

We do not stop your server after a certain amount of time, and we aim for 99% or more uptime on nodes and panels.

5. Do you have support?

We have over 1 support agent online on average and try to provide the best support possible! We also offer coding help to paid customers for NodeJS and Python.
